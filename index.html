Research Profile

My research develops theoretically rigorous and computationally scalable methodologies in small area estimation, spatial statistics, machine learning, and uncertainty quantification.

Current work focuses on neural tangent kernel-based estimation, spatio-temporal multivariate modelling, climate vulnerability mapping, and advanced statistical learning approaches for official statistics.

The research integrates asymptotic theory, optimization algorithms, mixed models, and statistical computing to address complex inferential problems.
